OUR VISION

Mission Statement

Perseverancia supports the education of all students and adolescents in El Salvador, through the Liceo Juan Calvino School with reliable instruction, resources and job opportunities after graduation.

Vision Statement

Perseverancia is working to change the perspective of education, reinventing the narrative of what education is like, allowing students to pursue their passions from day one. Through Juan Calvino, creating the possibility of a third cycle (7-9) and a future technical school to focus on improving the quality of life of families with limited resources. Focusing on practical training, creating the basis for a better future for the community.
